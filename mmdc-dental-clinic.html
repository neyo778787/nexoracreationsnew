MMDC UAE Dental Clinic

How we redesigned the web portal for a premium dental clinic in the United Arab Emirates, resulting in a booking surge and streamlined user operations.

Client

MMDC UAE

Industry

Dental Healthcare

Service

UI/UX & Web Development

Timeline

8 Weeks

The Challenge

MMDC Dental Clinic was facing drop-offs in patient booking numbers due to an outdated, slow, and non-responsive landing page. Patients found it difficult to explore available clinic services or book specific dental specialist slots from their mobile devices, leading to increased call volumes and loss of online conversions.

The Solution

Our team developed a clean, modern, and speed-optimized Next.js architecture from scratch. We focused heavily on mobile responsiveness and visual hierarchy. We designed clear, accessible specialist profiles and created a simplified 3-step slot booking checkout. By incorporating local schema markups, we also optimized SEO configurations to attract organic regional search volumes.

Key Implementations

  • Modular Navigation: Intuitive menus structured to guide users straight to appointment setups.
  • Next.js Engine: Implementing Server-Side Rendering (SSR) for instantaneous page loads.
  • Figma Design: Whitespace-rich, clean healthcare visual layouts conveying premium medical trust.

Performance Outcomes

The post-launch audits showed immediately visible growth across all primary patient engagement paths:

Booking conversions

-45%

96%
