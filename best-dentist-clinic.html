Healthcare Landing Page

Best Dentist Clinic

Optimizing visual layouts and booking structures on a targeted landing page for local emergency dental services, focusing on trust signals.

Client

Best Dentist Group

Industry

Medical Services

Service

UI/UX Design & Webflow Development

Timeline

4 Weeks

The Challenge

The client was running Google Ad campaigns targeting local dental services, but was experiencing a high cost-per-acquisition (CPA). The landing page was confusing, lacked clear trust signals, and had a complex multi-page form that caused 65% of mobile users to exit before completion.

The Solution

We designed a high-converting, single-page Webflow landing page focusing on instant value delivery. We moved emergency phone options to a sticky footer, placed dental testimonials prominently in the hero layout, and introduced a 10-second booking form. We built detailed FAQ accordions to handle patient objections and integrated schema tags to support local search SEO ranking.

Key Implementations

  • Sticky Mobil Call CTA: Ensuring emergency patients can click to dial instantly.
  • Trust Seals: Showcasing doctor profiles and verified patient reviews directly above the fold.
  • Performance Code: Stripping unused scripts to make the page load under 0.8 seconds.

Performance Outcomes

Our redesign immediately lowered advertising acquisition costs while boosting booking conversions:
