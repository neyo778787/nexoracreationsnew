Common Questions

What is your typical project delivery timeline?

+

Typical corporate website redesign timelines run between 4 to 8 weeks depending on the complexity of design iterations, number of pages, and technical database configurations. Full custom e-commerce applications generally take 8 to 12 weeks from discovery to deployment.

Do you provide SEO Audits for existing domains?

+

Yes, we perform complete SEO Domain Audits checking search visibility, key terms mappings, link profiles, and crawler bottlenecks. This audit is completely free for prospective corporate partners planning full site overhauls.

What payment milestones do you offer?

+

We split project costs across four milestones: 25% on agreement and wireframing launch, 25% on design layout completion approvals, 25% on development environment build review, and 25% upon testing audits and domain launch.

Can we edit page text and articles internally?

+

Absolutely. We build modular, custom CMS structures (like Webflow, Headless CMS, or Custom Dashboards) enabling team members to create blog posts, add portfolio case studies, and change page text easily without writing code.
